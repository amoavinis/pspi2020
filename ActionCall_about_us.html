What is this website's purpose?

In this website, we encourage people to contribute to the well-being of their community by taking part in various voluntary events. These activities are announced in our forum by our members, and are open for everyone to contribute to. Such activities include environmental cleanups, Stray animal care, vandalism restoration, and other acts for the purpose of making the world a better place.
Last but not least, we want to let people know about some of this country's major organizations and there that are already fighting for this purpose, As well as their contributions so far.
